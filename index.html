Intern Name


Hey everyone, this's Yogender.I did my internship with Sudaksh organization in December'2019. My experience is 10/10. I wasn't expecting this to an internship but this happened; be it traveling, be it food, be it the place we were residing, everything was so damn good! Talking about when we were going to public interactions, nukad naatak, plastic rallies, restoration of the parks and telling the people what is the difference that exists between the two dustbins, it was a difficult task, but when it is about to team it's nothing difficult. There's a difference between all time work in work and play in play but what I learnt in this internship is that work and play exist together. Thank you so much Sudaksh organization, thank you so much Anusha di for selecting me; and a big thumbs up to the team leader Mohit sir. I thank him. Under his guidance, we were able to do what we desired to. Everyone winning for the opportunity, do grab it, don't loose it, you'll regret it; and I give two big thumbs up to this organization, thank you so much.
